Harry Styles

The biggest pop star of our generation

The following list is a time line of Harry Style's life so far

  • 1994 - Born in Redditch, Worcestershire, England.
  • 2010 - Harry auditioned for British Singing Competition show, The X-Factor.
  • 2010 - Harry was put into a group formed by Simon Cowell, called One Direction.
  • 2011 - One Direction releases their first single, "That's What Makes You Beautiful," and later their debut album Up All Night. Up All Night debuts number one in the United States.
  • 2012 - One Direction relase their second album, Take Me Home.
  • 2013 - One Direction release their third album, Midnight Memories.
  • 2014 - One Direction release their fourth Four.
  • 2015 - One Direction release their fifth and final album, Made in the A.M.
  • 2016 - Styles signs a recording contact with Columbia Records.
  • 2016 - Styles makes his debut televised performance as a solo artist on Saturday Night Live, performing his two singles, "Sign of The Times" and "Ever Since New York."
  • 2017 - Styles releases his self-titled debut album, Harry Styles.
  • 2017 - Styles begins his first headlining tour, Harry Styles: Live On Tour. This tour visits North and South Americas, Europe, Asia, and Australia and ends in July 2018.
  • 2017 - Styles made his debut as an actor in Christopher Nolan's war film, Dunkirk, playing a British solider named Alex.
  • 2018 - Styles begins modelling for Gucci, appearing in several brand campaigns.
  • 2019 - Styles releases his second solo album, Fine Line, including 5 hit singles, "Adore You", "Watermelon Sugar", "Falling", "Golden", and "Treat People With Kindness".
  • 2021 - Harry Styles embarks on his second headlining tour, Love On Tour, which was rescheduled in 2020 due to the COVID-19 Pandemic.
  • 2021 - Styles makes a cameo in Eternals as Eros, brother of Thanos.
  • 2021 - He launches his gender neutral skin and nail care band, Pleasing, in November 2021.
  • 2022 - Styles releases his third and most recent album, Harry's House. This record becomes the fastest selling record of 2022 (so far).
  • 2022 - Styles stars in Olivia Wilde's "Don't Worry Darling" alongside Florence Pugh.
  • 2022 - Styles also stars in My Policeman, alongside Emma Corrin.
